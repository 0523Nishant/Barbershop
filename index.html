About Us

Discover the tradition and craftsmanship behind PMC Barber

Traditional Barbering With A Modern Touch

Founded in 2015, PMC Barber has established itself as a premier destination for men's grooming in the heart of the city. Our barbershop combines old-world charm with contemporary style to create a unique experience.

We believe that a great haircut is more than just a service - it's an art form. Our master barbers undergo rigorous training to perfect their craft, ensuring every client leaves looking and feeling their best.
